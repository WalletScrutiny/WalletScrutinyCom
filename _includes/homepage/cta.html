Security starts with scrutiny.

Not everyone is a developer. Not everyone has to be.

There’s more to protecting your bitcoin than securing your seed phase. While some wallets claim to be open-source, secure, and non-custodial, that’s not something the average user can easily verify. Wallet Scrutiny audits wallets for these and other potential security flaws so that you don’t have to.
